---
title: "Ad spend optimization"
description: "Prompts for getting more revenue from your advertising budget."
---

<Note>
Best with: **Meta Ads**, **Google Ads**, **TikTok Ads**, **Shopify**
</Note>

### Efficiency

> "What's our blended ROAS across all paid channels? How does it compare to last month?"

> "Which campaigns are underperforming? Show me anything with ROAS below 2x that's spent more than $500 in the last 30 days."

> "Where are we wasting ad spend? Show me ad sets with high spend but low conversion rates."

> "What's our CAC (customer acquisition cost — total ad spend divided by new customers acquired) by channel this month?"

### Scaling

> "Which campaigns have room to scale — meaning strong ROAS with low impression share or small audience reach?"

> "If I had an extra $10,000 in ad budget this month, where would it generate the best return based on current performance?"

> "Are any campaigns hitting diminishing returns? Show me where ROAS has been declining as spend increased."

### Attribution

> "Compare what Meta reports as revenue vs what we actually see in Shopify for the same period."

> "How does our Fairing survey attribution ('How did you hear about us?') compare to what the ad platforms report?"

> "What percentage of our attributed revenue comes from branded vs non-branded search terms?"
