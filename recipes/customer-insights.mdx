---
title: "Customer insights"
description: "Prompts for understanding who your customers are and how they behave."
---

<Note>
Best with: **Shopify**, **Google Analytics**, **Gorgias**, **Okendo**
</Note>

### Who are your customers?

> "What does our average customer look like? Show me average order value, orders per customer, and lifetime value."

> "How is our customer base growing? Compare new vs returning customer revenue month over month for the last 6 months."

> "Which customer segments are growing fastest? Break down by acquisition channel."

### Buying behavior

> "What's our repeat purchase rate over the last 90 days? How does that compare to the previous 90 days?"

> "What's the average time between a customer's first and second purchase?"

> "Which products are most commonly purchased together? Show me the top 10 product pairs."

> "What product do most first-time buyers purchase?"

### Voice of the customer

> "What are the most common themes in our product reviews this quarter? Group by positive and negative."

> "What are customers contacting support about most? Are there any product-specific issues driving ticket volume?"

> "What language do our happiest customers use in their reviews? Show me the most common phrases in 5-star reviews."
