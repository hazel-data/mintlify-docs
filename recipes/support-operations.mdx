---
title: "Support operations"
description: "Prompts for monitoring support performance and identifying trends."
---

<Note>
Best with: **Gorgias** or **Zendesk**
</Note>

### Team performance

> "What's our average first response time this month? How does it compare to our target?"

> "Show me CSAT (customer satisfaction) scores by agent for the last 30 days."

> "Which agents have the fastest resolution times? Show me a leaderboard."

> "What's our current ticket backlog? How many tickets are older than 48 hours?"

### Trends & issues

> "What are the most common support ticket topics this month? Compare to last month."

> "How has our ticket volume trended over the last 90 days? Flag any spikes."

> "Which products are generating the most support tickets? Is there a quality or sizing issue?"
