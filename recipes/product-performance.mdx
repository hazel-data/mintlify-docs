---
title: "Product performance"
description: "Prompts for analyzing your product catalog, inventory, and merchandising."
---

<Note>
Best with: **Shopify**, **Okendo**
</Note>

### Best sellers & revenue

> "Show me our top 20 products by revenue this month. Include units sold and average selling price."

> "Break down revenue by product category for the last quarter. Which categories are growing vs declining?"

> "How are our newest products performing? Show revenue and units for anything launched in the last 60 days."

### Inventory & operations

> "Which products are at risk of going out of stock in the next 30 days based on current sell-through rate?"

> "What are our return rates by product? Flag anything above 10%."

> "What are the top reasons for returns? Break it down by product category."

### Product strategy

> "Which products have the highest customer lifetime value — meaning customers who buy them go on to spend the most over time?"

> "Are there products we should consider bundling? Show me items frequently purchased together."

> "Which products are declining in sales compared to last quarter? Show the trend."

> "What's the revenue impact of our hero product? What percentage of total revenue does it represent?"
