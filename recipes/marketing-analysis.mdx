Best with: **Meta Ads**, **Google Ads**, **Klaviyo**, **Google Analytics**
</Note>

### Channel performance

> "What's our blended ROAS (return on ad spend — total revenue divided by total ad spend) across all paid channels this month?"

> "Compare performance across all our marketing channels for the last 30 days. Show spend, revenue, ROAS, and new customer count for each."

> "Which channel is driving the most new customer acquisitions this quarter? Break it down by first-order revenue."

### Email & SMS

> "What percentage of our total revenue comes from email this month?"

> "Show me our top 5 Klaviyo flows by revenue. How do they compare to last month?"

> "How are our email open rates trending over the last 90 days? Flag any flows with declining engagement."

> "Compare email revenue vs SMS revenue for the last quarter."

### Campaign deep dives

> "Which Meta campaigns have a ROAS above 3x in the last 30 days? Show spend and revenue for each."

> "What are our top 10 Google Ads keywords by conversion rate? Include cost per click and total spend."

> "Show me any campaigns where CPA (cost per acquisition — what we pay to get one new customer) increased more than 20% month over month."
