### Monday morning review

> "Give me a complete business review for last week. Cover revenue, orders, marketing spend, ROAS, and any anomalies. Compare everything to the week before."

> "What were our biggest wins last week? What should I be concerned about?"

> "How does last week compare to the same week last year?"

### Team updates

> "Give me a 3-bullet summary of our marketing performance last week that I can share with the team."

> "Write a brief leadership update covering revenue, growth, and key metrics for last week."

> "What are the top 5 KPIs the board would want to see for this month so far?"

### Making it recurring

<Tip>
Love one of these prompts? When scheduled reports launch, you'll be able to turn any of these into an automated report delivered on your schedule. In the meantime, pin your review thread (hover over it in the side nav → three dots → Pin) to come back to it each week.
</Tip>
