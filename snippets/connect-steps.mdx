---
title: "Connect steps"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Click **Integrations** in the side nav. You'll see a list of all available data sources.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find your integration">
    Search by name or browse by category (Marketing, Sales, Finance, Support, Reviews).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Connect and authorize">
    Hit **Connect** and follow the prompts — either sign in through the platform (OAuth) or paste an API key. It takes about 30 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for your data">
    Hazel starts pulling in your historical data right away. After connecting, Hazel handles all data cleaning and setup — no manual configuration needed. The initial backfill can take a few hours to a few days depending on the source's rate limits and data volume. You can start asking questions about recent data almost immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
