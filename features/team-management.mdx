## Built for your whole team

Hazel isn't a single-player tool. Invite your marketing, CX, merchandising, and leadership team — everyone gets their own conversations, their own [Memory](/features/memory), and their own way of working with your data.

The best part? No per-seat pricing. Invite as many people as you want.

## Inviting team members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Click **Team** in the side nav.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click the **Invite** button and enter one or more email addresses. They'll get an email invitation to join your Hazel organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Everyone on your team gets the same full Hazel experience — chat, reports, memory, everything.

## Experimental Access

Some users may be granted **Experimental Access** by the Hazel team. This gives early access to new features before general availability — talk to us if you're interested.

## No per-seat pricing

Hazel charges a flat annual fee. Invite five people or fifty — the price stays the same. No surprise bills when your team grows, and no awkward conversations about who "deserves" a seat.

Everyone who needs data answers should have access. That's the whole point.

<Tip>
  Encourage each team member to set up their own User Memory with their role and preferences. A marketing manager and a CX lead will get different value from the same data — Memory is how Hazel tailors the experience to each person.
</Tip>
