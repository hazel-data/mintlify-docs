## What is Hazel?

Hazel is an AI-powered operating system for ecommerce brands. It connects to your data sources — Shopify, Meta Ads, Google Analytics, Klaviyo, and many more — and lets you ask questions about your business in plain English.

Instead of digging through dashboards or waiting for someone to pull a report, you just ask. "What was our revenue last week?" "Which Meta campaigns have the best ROAS (return on ad spend)?" "How is our email retention performing?"

Hazel gives you answers with charts, tables, and inline citations so you can verify every number. No SQL, no spreadsheets, no analyst backlog.

## Who is Hazel for?

Hazel is built for the people who need data answers every day but don't want to write queries or build dashboards to get them.

- **Marketing managers** who want to check campaign performance without logging into five platforms
- **Ecommerce directors** who need a clear picture of revenue, orders, and trends
- **Customer success leads** tracking support volume, satisfaction, and resolution times
- **Founders** who want to understand their business without waiting for a weekly report

If you've ever thought "I just need a quick answer" and then spent 30 minutes hunting through tabs, Hazel is for you.
