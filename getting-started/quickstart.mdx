## Get started in 5 minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your organization">
    Head to [app.fromhazel.ai](https://app.fromhazel.ai) and sign up. You'll name your organization — this is usually your brand or company name.

    Once you're in, you'll land on the chat screen. That's home base.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Shopify">
    Click **Integrations** in the side nav and find Shopify. Click **Connect** and authorize Hazel to access your store.

    Your historical data starts backfilling immediately. You'll be able to ask questions about recent data right away, and the full backfill can take a few hours to a few days depending on the source's rate limits and data volume. Hazel handles all data cleaning and setup — no manual configuration needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add more integrations">
    While Shopify backfills, connect your other platforms. Meta Ads, Google Analytics, Klaviyo, and 80+ others are available. Each one takes about 30 seconds — just click **Connect** and authorize.

    The more sources you connect, the more complete your answers will be. Hazel works best when it can see your full picture.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up Memory">
    Click **Memory** in the side nav and teach Hazel about your brand. This is where you add your metric definitions, product categories, and business rules.

    For example, you might add: "We consider a customer 'active' if they've placed an order in the last 120 days" or "Our hero product is the Classic Bundle." This helps Hazel give you answers that match how your team actually talks about your business.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Ask your first question

Head back to the chat screen and try one of these to see Hazel in action:

- "What was our revenue last week compared to the week before?"
- "Show me our top 10 products by revenue this month"
- "How are our Meta ad campaigns performing this quarter?"
- "What's our repeat purchase rate over the last 90 days?"

Don't worry about phrasing things perfectly. Hazel understands natural language, so ask the way you'd ask a coworker.

<Tip>
  **How fresh is my data?** Your data refreshes every 4 hours automatically. When you first connect a source, Hazel backfills your historical data — this can take a few hours to a few days depending on the source's rate limits and data volume. You don't need to do anything; it happens in the background.
</Tip>
