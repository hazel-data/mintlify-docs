## Overview

Yotpo helps brands collect and display product reviews, ratings, and visual UGC. Connect it to Hazel to analyze customer feedback alongside your sales and marketing data.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Yotpo, Hazel has access to:

- **Reviews** — every product review with star ratings, text, and timestamps
- **Ratings** — aggregate ratings by product
- **Review metadata** — reviewer information and review dates

## How to connect

Find Yotpo in the [Hazel marketplace](https://app.fromhazel.ai/integrations/marketplace?search=yotpo) and click the **+** button. You'll need to provide your Yotpo API token and Store ID.

**What you'll need:** Your Yotpo API token (utoken) and Store ID, found in your Yotpo account settings.

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Yotpo data is connected, try questions like these:

- "What's our average star rating by product?"
- "Which products have the most reviews?"
- "Show me review volume trends this quarter"
- "Which products have the lowest ratings?"
- "How many reviews did we collect last month?"

## Good to know

- Pair with Shopify to see how review ratings correlate with sales performance and return rates.
- Your data refreshes automatically.
