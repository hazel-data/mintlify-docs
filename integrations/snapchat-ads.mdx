## Overview

Bring your Snapchat Ads data into Hazel to track campaign performance alongside your other paid channels. See spend, engagement, and how Snapchat fits into your overall media mix.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Snapchat Ads, Hazel has access to:

- **Campaigns, ad squads, and ads** — your full account structure with status and settings
- **Spend** — daily spend at the campaign level
- **Engagement metrics** — impressions and clicks
- **Device breakdowns** — how your ads perform across different mobile devices

## How to connect

Find Snapchat Ads in the [Hazel marketplace](https://app.fromhazel.ai/integrations/marketplace?search=snapchat+ads) and click the **+** button. You'll sign in through Snapchat in a popup window to authorize the connection.

**What you'll need:** A Snapchat Business Account with access to your ad campaigns.

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Snapchat Ads data is connected, try questions like these:

- "How much are we spending on Snapchat vs other channels?"
- "What's our Snapchat CPC this month?"
- "Show me our top Snapchat campaigns by impressions"
- "How has our Snapchat spend trended over the last quarter?"
- "Compare Snapchat vs TikTok ad performance"

## Good to know

- Pair with your other ad platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok, Pinterest) to see where Snapchat fits in your full paid media mix.
- Your data refreshes automatically.
