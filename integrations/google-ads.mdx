## Overview

Bring your Google Ads data into Hazel to analyze Search, Shopping, Display, and Performance Max campaigns alongside your other channels. See what's working, what's wasting budget, and how Google fits into your overall marketing mix.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Google Ads, Hazel has access to:

- **Campaigns and ad groups** — your full account structure with status and budget settings
- **Keywords** — keyword performance, match types, and quality scores
- **Search terms** — the actual searches people type before clicking your ads
- **Spend and conversions** — daily spend, conversion counts, and conversion value
- **Impression share** — how often your ads show up compared to how often they could
- **Quality score** — Google's rating of your keyword relevance and landing page experience

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Integrations">
    Open **Settings** in Hazel and click **Integrations**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Google Ads and click Connect">
    Search for Google Ads or find it in the Marketing category.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with your Google account">
    You'll be redirected to Google to authorize the connection. Use the account that has access to your Google Ads.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Google Ads data is connected, try questions like these:

- "What are our top converting keywords?"
- "Show me campaigns with declining ROAS"
- "What's our Google Ads spend vs revenue this quarter?"
- "Which search terms are wasting budget?"
- "Compare Search vs Shopping campaign performance"
- "What's our impression share for branded keywords?"

## Good to know

- Hazel supports **Search, Shopping, Display, and Performance Max** campaigns.
- Pair with Meta Ads and Shopify to get a complete picture of your paid acquisition performance across channels.
- Your data refreshes every 4 hours automatically.
