## Overview

Attentive is a leading SMS marketing platform for consumer brands. Connect it to Hazel to see how your text message campaigns perform, track subscriber growth, and understand how much revenue SMS drives for your business.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Attentive, Hazel has access to:

- **Campaigns** — every SMS campaign with delivery, click, and conversion data
- **Messages** — individual message performance and content
- **Subscribers** — your SMS subscriber list with growth trends and opt-in/opt-out data
- **Events** — subscriber activity like clicks, conversions, and revenue events
- **Revenue attribution** — how much revenue each SMS campaign and message generates

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Attentive data is connected, try questions like these:

- "How much revenue are we generating from SMS?"
- "What's our SMS subscriber growth rate?"
- "Compare SMS vs email revenue this quarter"
- "Which SMS campaigns had the highest conversion rate?"
- "How many subscribers did we add last month?"

## Good to know

- Pair with Klaviyo to compare SMS and email performance across both platforms.
- Revenue attribution lets you see exactly which texts are driving sales.
- Your data refreshes every 4 hours automatically.
