## All your data, one conversation

Hazel connects to 80+ platforms across marketing, sales, finance, support, and reviews. Connect once, and Hazel keeps your data synced automatically — so you can ask questions that span every part of your business.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your account">
    Authorize via OAuth (a secure sign-in popup) or paste an API key. Either way, it takes about 30 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hazel backfills your historical data">
    As soon as you connect, Hazel starts pulling in your historical data. This usually takes a few hours depending on the volume.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Stay in sync automatically">
    Your data refreshes every 4 hours. No manual imports, no spreadsheets — just always-fresh data ready for your questions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
