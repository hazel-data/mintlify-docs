## Overview

Tracksuit measures how well consumers know and consider your brand. Connect it to Hazel to track your brand health metrics — awareness, consideration, and purchase intent — alongside your performance marketing data.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Tracksuit, Hazel has access to:

- **Funnel metrics** — brand awareness, consideration, and purchase intent over time
- **Brand health trends** — how your brand metrics are changing period over period

## How to connect

Find Tracksuit in the [Hazel marketplace](https://app.fromhazel.ai/integrations/marketplace?search=tracksuit) and click the **+** button. You'll need to paste your Tracksuit API key.

**What you'll need:** Your Tracksuit API key, found in your Tracksuit account settings.

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Tracksuit data is connected, try questions like these:

- "What's our current brand awareness?"
- "How has our brand consideration trended over the last 6 months?"
- "Show me our full brand funnel — awareness to consideration to purchase intent"
- "How does our brand awareness compare to last quarter?"

## Good to know

- Tracksuit data is survey-based, so metrics represent consumer perception rather than behavioral data.
- Pair with your ad platforms to see whether brand marketing spend is translating into awareness and consideration gains.
- Your data refreshes automatically.
