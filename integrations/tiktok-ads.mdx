## Overview

Bring your TikTok Ads data into Hazel to see how your TikTok campaigns perform alongside your other paid channels. Analyze spend, conversions, and creative performance without juggling multiple dashboards.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect TikTok Ads, Hazel has access to:

- **Campaigns, ad groups, and ads** — your full account structure with status and budget settings
- **Spend** — daily and lifetime spend at every level
- **Impressions and clicks** — reach and engagement metrics
- **Conversions** — conversion counts and value from your TikTok pixel
- **Video views** — view counts, completion rates, and video engagement metrics

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Integrations">
    Open **Settings** in Hazel and click **Integrations**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find TikTok Ads and click Connect">
    Search for TikTok Ads or find it in the Marketing category.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in through TikTok">
    You'll be redirected to TikTok to authorize the connection. Use the account that has access to your TikTok Ads Manager.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example questions you can ask

Once your TikTok Ads data is connected, try questions like these:

- "How are our TikTok campaigns performing vs Meta?"
- "What's our TikTok CPA this month?"
- "Show me our top TikTok ads by conversion rate"
- "How much are we spending on TikTok vs other channels?"
- "What's our TikTok ROAS trend over the last 90 days?"

## Good to know

- Pair with Meta Ads and Google Ads to compare performance across all your paid channels in one question.
- Your data refreshes every 4 hours automatically.
