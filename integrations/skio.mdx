## Overview

Skio is a modern subscription management platform for Shopify brands. Connect it to Hazel to track your subscription health — active subscribers, cancellation trends, and detailed insights from Skio's cancel flow.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Skio, Hazel has access to:

- **Subscriptions** — every subscription with status, cadence, and line items
- **Orders** — subscription-generated orders and their details
- **Cancel flow sessions** — when a subscriber starts the cancellation process, including whether they completed it or were retained
- **Cancellation reasons** — documented reasons from Skio's cancel flow

## How to connect

Find Skio in the [Hazel marketplace](https://app.fromhazel.ai/integrations/marketplace?search=skio) and click the **+** button. You'll need to paste your Skio API key.

**What you'll need:** Your Skio API key, found in your Skio account settings.

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Skio data is connected, try questions like these:

- "How many active subscriptions do we have?"
- "What's our subscription churn rate?"
- "What are the top cancellation reasons from our cancel flow?"
- "How many subscribers started the cancel flow but were retained?"
- "Show me subscription revenue trend over the last quarter"
- "Which subscription products have the best retention?"

## Good to know

- **Cancel flow insights** are one of the most valuable parts of this integration — you can see exactly why people leave and how effective your retention offers are.
- Cancel flow data is only available when subscribers go through Skio's cancel process (direct cancellations may not capture a reason).
- Pair with Shopify to see the full picture of subscription vs. one-time purchase revenue.
- Your data refreshes automatically.
