## Overview

Recharge is the leading subscription management platform for Shopify brands. Connect it to Hazel to understand your subscription business — active subscribers, churn trends, revenue from recurring orders, and what's driving cancellations.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Recharge, Hazel has access to:

- **Subscriptions** — every subscription with status, cadence (weekly, monthly, etc.), pricing, and lifecycle events
- **Charges** — billing events including successful charges, failed charges, and skipped charges
- **Customers** — subscriber profiles with payment methods and addresses
- **Cancellation reasons** — why subscribers cancel, including any comments they leave
- **Bundles** — bundle subscription structure and item selections
- **Status history** — how subscriptions change over time (active → paused → cancelled, product swaps, price changes)

## How to connect

Find Recharge in the [Hazel marketplace](https://app.fromhazel.ai/integrations/marketplace?search=recharge) and click the **+** button. You'll need to paste your Recharge API token.

**What you'll need:** Your Recharge API token, found in your Recharge admin under API tokens.

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Recharge data is connected, try questions like these:

- "How many active subscriptions do we have?"
- "What's our subscription churn rate this month?"
- "What are the top reasons people cancel their subscriptions?"
- "Show me MRR trend over the last 6 months"
- "What's the average subscription lifetime before cancellation?"
- "Which products have the highest subscription retention rate?"
- "How many subscriptions were paused vs cancelled last month?"

## Good to know

- Pair with Shopify to see how subscription revenue compares to one-time purchases.
- **Bundle subscriptions** are tracked separately — Hazel knows which subscriptions are bundles so your counts stay accurate.
- Subscription status changes (paused, swapped, cancelled) are tracked over time, so you can analyze the full lifecycle.
- Your data refreshes automatically.
