## Overview

See your Meta (Facebook + Instagram) ad performance alongside your Shopify revenue. Hazel pulls in your campaign data so you can ask about spend, ROAS, and performance without ever opening Ads Manager.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Meta Ads, Hazel has access to:

- **Campaigns, ad sets, and ads** — your full account structure with status and settings
- **Spend** — daily and lifetime spend at every level
- **ROAS** — return on ad spend (how much revenue you generate per dollar spent on ads)
- **CPM, CPC, and CPA** — cost per thousand impressions, cost per click, and cost per acquisition
- **Demographic breakdowns** — performance by age, gender, and location
- **Placement breakdowns** — how your ads perform on Facebook vs Instagram, Feed vs Stories, and more

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Integrations">
    Open **Settings** in Hazel and click **Integrations**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Meta Ads and click Connect">
    Search for Meta Ads or find it in the Marketing category.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in through Meta Business Manager">
    You'll be redirected to Meta to authorize the connection. Make sure you sign in with an account that has access to the ad accounts you want to analyze.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Meta Ads data is connected, try questions like these:

- "What's our Meta ROAS this month?"
- "Which campaigns have ROAS above 3x?"
- "Show me spend and revenue by campaign for the last 30 days"
- "Compare our Facebook vs Instagram ad performance"
- "Which ad sets have the highest CPA?"
- "How has our Meta spend trended over the last quarter?"

## Good to know

<Note>
  You'll need **Meta Business Manager** access to connect. If you work with an agency, make sure they've granted your account the right permissions.
</Note>

- Data includes both Facebook and Instagram — you can analyze them together or separately.
- Pair with Shopify to compare Meta-reported ROAS against actual Shopify revenue for a more accurate picture.
- Your data refreshes every 4 hours automatically.
