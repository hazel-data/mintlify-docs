## Overview

Bring your Pinterest Ads data into Hazel to see how your Pinterest campaigns perform alongside your other paid channels. Analyze spend, keyword performance, and engagement without switching between ad dashboards.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Pinterest Ads, Hazel has access to:

- **Campaigns** — your full campaign structure with status and budget settings
- **Keywords** — keyword-level performance data
- **Spend** — daily spend at the campaign level
- **Engagement metrics** — impressions, clicks, and video views
- **Performance trends** — daily metrics for tracking campaign health over time

## How to connect

Find Pinterest Ads in the [Hazel marketplace](https://app.fromhazel.ai/integrations/marketplace?search=pinterest+ads) and click the **+** button. You'll sign in through Pinterest in a popup window to authorize the connection.

**What you'll need:** A Pinterest Business Account with access to your ad campaigns.

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Pinterest Ads data is connected, try questions like these:

- "How is our Pinterest ad spend performing vs other channels?"
- "What's our Pinterest CPC this month?"
- "Show me our top Pinterest campaigns by clicks"
- "How has our Pinterest spend trended over the last quarter?"
- "Compare Pinterest vs Meta ad performance"

## Good to know

- Pair with Meta Ads, Google Ads, and your other ad platforms to compare Pinterest performance in the context of your full paid media mix.
- Your data refreshes automatically.
