## Overview

Okendo helps DTC brands collect and display product reviews. Connect it to Hazel to analyze ratings, review sentiment, and customer feedback alongside your sales and marketing data.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Okendo, Hazel has access to:

- **Reviews** — every product review with star ratings, text, and timestamps
- **Ratings** — aggregate ratings by product
- **Products** — your product catalog as mapped in Okendo
- **Review attributes** — structured feedback like fit, quality, and value ratings
- **Media** — photos and videos attached to reviews

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Integrations">
    Open **Settings** in Hazel and click **Integrations**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Okendo and click Connect">
    Search for Okendo or find it in the Reviews category.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste your Okendo API key">
    In Okendo, find your API key in your account settings. Copy it and paste it into Hazel to complete the connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Okendo data is connected, try questions like these:

- "What's our average star rating by product?"
- "Show me review themes for our top products"
- "Which products have the most negative reviews?"
- "How has our review volume trended this quarter?"
- "What do customers say about sizing in their reviews?"
- "Which products have improved their ratings over time?"

## Good to know

- Pair with Shopify to see how review ratings correlate with sales performance and return rates.
- Review attributes give you structured feedback beyond star ratings — like how customers rate fit, quality, or value.
- Your data refreshes every 4 hours automatically.
