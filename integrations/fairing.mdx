## Overview

Fairing (formerly Enquire Labs) captures post-purchase survey responses — the "How did you hear about us?" question and more. Connect it to Hazel to compare what customers say with what your ad platforms report, and get a clearer picture of what's actually driving sales.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Fairing, Hazel has access to:

- **Survey responses** — answers to every survey question you've asked
- **Attribution answers** — responses to "How did you hear about us?" and similar discovery questions
- **NPS scores** — Net Promoter Score data (a measure of how likely customers are to recommend you, on a scale of 0-10)
- **Custom question responses** — answers to any custom questions you've added to your surveys

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Integrations">
    Open **Settings** in Hazel and click **Integrations**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Fairing and click Connect">
    Search for Fairing or find it in the Reviews & Surveys category.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste your Fairing API key">
    In Fairing, find your API key in your account settings. Copy it and paste it into Hazel to complete the connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Fairing data is connected, try questions like these:

- "What are customers saying about how they found us?"
- "Show me our NPS score trend"
- "Compare Fairing attribution data to ad platform reported data"
- "What percentage of customers say they found us through a friend?"
- "How has our NPS changed over the last 6 months?"
- "Which acquisition channels do first-time buyers report most often?"

## Good to know

<Tip>
  **Attribution comparison** is one of the most powerful uses of Fairing data. Compare what customers tell you (survey responses) with what ad platforms claim (click-based attribution) to understand where your money is really working.
</Tip>

- **NPS** stands for Net Promoter Score. It measures how likely customers are to recommend you, on a scale of 0-10. Scores of 9-10 are "promoters," 7-8 are "passives," and 0-6 are "detractors."
- Pair with Shopify and your ad platforms for the most complete attribution picture.
- Your data refreshes every 4 hours automatically.
