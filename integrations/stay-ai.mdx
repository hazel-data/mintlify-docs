## Overview

Stay AI is a Shopify-native subscription management platform with built-in churn risk scoring. Connect it to Hazel to track your subscription health — active subscribers, who's at risk of churning, dunning state, and the products driving recurring revenue.

## What Hazel pulls

Once you connect Stay AI, Hazel has access to:

- **Subscriptions** — every subscription with status, billing cadence, pricing, and lifecycle events (active, paused, cancelled)
- **Subscription items** — the products and variants on each subscription, including quantity, SKU, and one-time add-ons
- **Recurring orders** — orders generated by subscription contracts and their line items
- **Churn risk** — Stay AI's churn risk score (0-1) and risk bucket for each active subscription
- **Dunning state** — when a subscription enters and exits the billing-retry cycle
- **Cancellation reasons** — the reason captured at cancellation time
- **Selling plan groups** — standard, prepaid, and calendar-prepaid plan configuration

## How to connect

Find Stay AI in the [Hazel marketplace](https://app.fromhazel.ai/integrations/marketplace?search=stay) and click the **+** button. You'll need to paste your Stay AI access token.

**What you'll need:** Your Stay AI access token, found in your Stay AI dashboard.

## Example questions you can ask

Once your Stay AI data is connected, try questions like these:

- "How many active Stay AI subscriptions do we have?"
- "What's our subscription churn rate this month?"
- "Which subscriptions are at risk of churning?"
- "What are the top reasons subscribers cancel?"
- "How many subscribers are currently in dunning?"
- "Which products have the highest subscription retention?"
- "What's the breakdown of standard vs prepaid subscriptions?"

## Good to know

- **Churn risk scoring** is one of the most useful parts of this integration — Stay AI assigns each subscription a 0-1 score plus a risk bucket so you can prioritize retention outreach.
- **Dunning tracking** lets you see exactly when subscriptions enter the billing-retry cycle and whether they recovered or churned.
- For revenue analysis, pair Stay AI with Shopify — subscription orders flow through Shopify as fulfillable orders, and Shopify is the definitive revenue record.
- Stay AI is a competitor to Recharge and Skio; brands typically run on only one. If you're migrating between platforms, both sources can coexist in Hazel during the transition.
- Your data refreshes automatically.
