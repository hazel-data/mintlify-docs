## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to know SQL or be technical to use Hazel?">
    Not at all. Hazel is designed for non-technical users. Ask questions in plain English, just like you'd ask a colleague. No spreadsheets, no formulas, no query language required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Hazel different from ChatGPT or Claude?">
    ChatGPT and Claude are general-purpose AI tools. Hazel is purpose-built for ecommerce — it's already connected to your data, understands ecommerce metrics natively, and remembers your brand between conversations. You don't need to upload CSVs or explain what ROAS means.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What AI model does Hazel use?">
    Hazel is built on Claude by Anthropic, one of the most capable AI models available.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. Hazel uses enterprise-grade encryption in transit and at rest. We never use your data to train AI models. Your data stays yours.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Data & integrations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How often does my data refresh?">
    Every 4 hours. When you first connect a source, Hazel backfills your historical data — this usually takes a few hours depending on the volume.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How far back does the historical data go?">
    Hazel pulls as much history as each platform provides. For Shopify, that's typically your entire order history. For ad platforms, it varies but usually goes back 1–2 years.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I connect multiple Shopify stores?">
    Yes. Each store connects separately and you can query across all of them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my integration isn't listed?">
    Contact us at support@fromhazel.ai. We add new integrations regularly and prioritize based on customer requests.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are inline citations?">
    Every number in Hazel's response has a clickable citation that shows you the underlying data. This lets you verify any answer and builds trust in the analysis. Learn more at [Inline citations](/features/citations).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Quick Ask and Deep Thinking?">
    Quick Ask gives you answers in seconds for straightforward questions. Deep Thinking takes a few minutes but runs a multi-step research process for complex, strategic questions. Learn more at [Quick Ask vs Deep Thinking](/features/quick-ask-vs-deep-thinking).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I share Hazel's responses with my team?">
    Yes. You can share any thread, and scheduled reports can be delivered to Slack channels or email distribution lists.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
