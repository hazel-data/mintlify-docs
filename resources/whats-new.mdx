<Update label="March 2026" description="Deep Thinking V2" tags={["Feature"]}>
  Deep Thinking just got a major upgrade. V2 delivers dramatically better analytical reasoning with structured outputs — tables, executive summaries, and key takeaways — instead of walls of text. Every statistic is now backed by [inline citations](/features/citations) so you can verify the data behind every finding.
</Update>

<Update label="March 2026" description="Inline Citations" tags={["Feature"]}>
  Every number Hazel gives you now includes a clickable citation. Click any citation to see the underlying query and data — so you can always verify where a number came from. This is especially powerful for Deep Thinking responses where complex multi-step analysis is involved.
</Update>

<Update label="February 2026" description="Scheduled Reports" tags={["Feature"]}>
  Turn any Hazel question into an automated report. Choose daily, weekly, or monthly delivery via Slack or email. Perfect for Monday morning metrics, weekly team updates, and monthly board reports. Create them through chat — just tell Hazel what you want and when. Learn more at [Scheduled reports](/features/scheduled-reports).
</Update>

<Update label="February 2026" description="TikTok Ads Integration" tags={["Integration"]}>
  TikTok Ads is now available as a data source. See your TikTok campaign performance alongside Meta, Google, and your other channels. Connect via **Settings > Integrations**.
</Update>
