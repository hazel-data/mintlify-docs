## Your data is safe with Hazel

We take data security seriously. Here's how Hazel protects your information.

### Encrypted everywhere

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Whether your data is moving between your tools and Hazel or sitting in our database, it's always protected.

### Never used for training

Your data is never used to train AI models — period. Your business data stays your business data.

### Isolated per organization

Every Hazel organization's data is logically isolated. Your data is never mixed with, accessible to, or visible to other customers.

### Your data stays yours

You can disconnect any integration at any time and your data will be removed. You're always in control of what Hazel has access to.
